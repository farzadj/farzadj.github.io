What the app does

Built for daily German recall, not generic flashcards.

Level workflows

Separate A1, A2, B1, B1+, and B2 paths with sentence, vocabulary, and grammar content.

Milestone journey

Each level includes guided milestones that break the content into manageable study blocks.

Offline persistence

Progress, favorites, hard cards, settings, and custom flashcards stay on-device.

Speech support

German speech playback with optional autoplay and device-based text-to-speech support.

Saved review

Favorite and hard cards can be reopened later as focused review decks.

Custom cards

Create your own sentence, vocabulary, or grammar-style flashcards inside the app.

Learning path

Five levels, one consistent system.

A1

Foundation

Core everyday vocabulary, first sentences, and beginner grammar practice.

A2

Basics in motion

Longer everyday dialogs, broader vocabulary, and more structured grammar review.

B1

Independent use

Topic-based communication, practical sentence recall, and richer vocabulary.

B1+

Expanded range

Larger decks, denser sentence work, and broader grammar coverage.

B2

Precision and control

Advanced sentence practice, nuanced vocabulary, and focused grammar reinforcement.

Privacy and Pro

Offline-first study, with optional Pro support.

Free version

Free users can study A1 and A2, open the first 3 milestones, download one optional pack/language, create 100 custom cards, and see basic study summaries.

Pro option

Unlocks B1-B2 levels, all milestones, Favorite and Hard decks, unlimited packs and languages, unlimited custom cards, and detailed performance analysis.

Local data

Study progress, saved cards, settings, and user-created cards are stored locally on the device.

No app backend

The app does not require a custom server to function. Network use is mainly for optional pack downloads, analytics, subscription, and app-distribution services.

What The App Does
Chart

Live chart workspace

Review native candlestick charts, switch strategy overlays, inspect setups, and plot entry, stop loss, and take profit directly on the chart.

Signals

Proposed and detected setups

Monitor current trade ideas, inspect rationale and caution notes, and review strategy signals grouped by lifecycle state.

Lab

Scanner, alerts, and historical checks

Compare live contexts, manage backend alert rules, reuse presets, and run historical checks with configurable strategy parameters.

Strategy Coverage
Strategy Stack

Built around practical setup families

Crypto Lab currently supports Order Blocks, Patterns, Break and Retest, Fair Value Gap, Liquidity Sweep, and Range Breakout workflows. Pattern coverage includes multiple chart structures such as Double Top, Double Bottom, Head and Shoulders, Inverse Head and Shoulders, Ascending Triangle, Descending Triangle, Symmetrical Triangle, Bull Flag, and Bear Flag.

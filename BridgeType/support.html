BridgeType Support

Help with setup, translation, keyboard behavior, privacy questions, and bug reports.

Keyboard setup

  • Install BridgeType from your Android device.
  • Open the app and follow the onboarding steps.
  • Enable BridgeType Keyboard in Android keyboard settings.
  • Choose BridgeType Keyboard while typing in a messenger app.
  • Download the ML Kit language models you need before offline translation.

Common limitations

  • Some messenger apps may restrict text selection, replacement, or sharing.
  • Voice recognition depends on your Android device and installed speech service.
  • ML Kit language models require network access when first downloaded.
  • BridgeType does not read private chat screens automatically for privacy and Play Store safety.

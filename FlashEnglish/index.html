What the app does

Built as a guided study workflow, not a generic card stack.

Level workflows

Dedicated A1, A2, B1, B2, and C1 libraries with sentence and vocabulary study paths.

Grammar workflow

Each level includes grammar practice sentences and grammar explanation topics.

Milestone journey

Guided milestone routes break each level into smaller, manageable study blocks.

Learner-language support

Study English with learner-language support content for Persian, Italian, Spanish, Russian, Arabic, Chinese, and French.

Speech and recall

Use English text-to-speech, progress tracking, and saved review to reinforce active recall.

Custom and saved cards

Create your own cards. Pro unlocks Favorite/Hard marking, saved decks, and level review.

Free and Pro access

Free includes A1, A2, the first 3 milestones, 100 custom cards, and basic completion summaries. Pro unlocks B1, B2, C1, all milestones, unlimited custom cards, saved review, and detailed analysis.

Learning path

Five visible levels, one consistent study system.

A1

Foundation

Core vocabulary, first sentence patterns, and beginner grammar practice.

A2

Everyday basics

Practical topic-based English, broader vocabulary, and stronger grammar control.

B1

Independent use

Longer sentence recall, more natural communication, and structured milestone progression.

B2

Upper intermediate precision

Nuanced vocabulary, more complex sentence structures, and higher recall demands.

C1

Advanced fluency

High-level English practice for advanced vocabulary, grammar, and sentence control.

Languages and access

English target study with multilingual learner support.

App interface

The app interface is available in English and German.

Learner-language support

Learner-side support is available across bundled content for Persian, Italian, Spanish, Russian, Arabic, Chinese, and French.

Offline-first study

Core study content, progress, saved cards, settings, and custom cards are designed to stay on the device.

Optional Pro subscription

The app uses an optional Google Play subscription for Pro access. FlashEnglish does not use ad-supported unlocks.

FlashEnglish Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the FlashEnglish Android application and the project website published at farzadj.github.io/FlashEnglish.

App package name: com.fj.flashenglish.

2. Data stored locally on your device

FlashEnglish is designed to work offline after installation. Most study information is stored locally on your device, including:

  • study progress and local session state
  • favorites and hard-card markers
  • settings such as theme, interface language, learner language, speech, and study preferences
  • milestone and workflow progress
  • user-created flashcards and local edits to bundled content

3. App languages and learner-language support

The app interface is available in English and German.

Learner-language support may be available for content such as prompts, examples, grammar explanations, and study text in Persian, Italian, Spanish, Russian, Arabic, Chinese, and French, depending on the bundled content.

4. Accounts

FlashEnglish does not require a custom FlashEnglish account to use its core study features.

5. Advertising and consent

FlashEnglish may use Google Mobile Ads, including interstitial and rewarded ad flows, depending on the feature and app configuration.

The app may also use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to manage consent where required.

6. Subscriptions and purchases

FlashEnglish includes an optional Google Play Billing integration for Pro access.

Payments, subscription management, and transaction handling are processed by Google Play, not by a custom FlashEnglish backend.

7. Speech and device services

The app can use text-to-speech services available on your Android device for English playback and, where supported, learner-language playback.

8. Network usage

Although FlashEnglish is offline-first, some flows may use the network for:

  • Google Mobile Ads
  • consent handling
  • Google Play Billing and subscription status through Google Play
  • app distribution and updates through Google Play

9. Third-party services

FlashEnglish may rely on third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Mobile Ads
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)
  • Google Play Billing
  • GitHub Pages for this website

10. Website hosting

The FlashEnglish website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process standard web-hosting data according to its own policies when you visit the website.

11. Data retention and deletion

Because most app data is stored locally, uninstalling the app or clearing app storage can remove that local data from your device.

12. Your choices

  • You can change theme, app language, learner language, and speech preferences inside the app.
  • You can choose whether to interact with optional rewarded-ad flows where they are offered.
  • You can choose whether to subscribe for Pro access through Google Play.

13. Children's privacy

FlashEnglish is an educational app. If children use the app, parents or guardians should review ad, subscription, and device privacy settings before use.

14. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app's features, third-party services, or data flows change. The updated version will be published on this page.

15. Contact

For project or privacy-related questions, use the project links below:

  • Support page
  • GitHub repository
  • GitHub issues

Common support topics

  • App install or update problems from Google Play or local builds
  • Offline deck import, workflow, or milestone progress behavior
  • Text-to-speech playback or device voice availability
  • Free/Pro access, Google Play subscription, and restore-purchase behavior
  • Flashcard corrections, grammar explanation feedback, or learner-language translation issues

Helpful details to include

When reporting a bug, include the app version, Android version, device model, and the exact screen or action that caused the issue.

If the issue relates to study content, include the level, content type, workflow, and the visible card or grammar topic text if possible.
